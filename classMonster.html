◆ monsterCd()

void Monster::monsterCd ( Monster &  target)

This is the main part of the cooldown counter program.

Parameters
targetIt is a monster type.

In this function we watch the cooldown counter, and we decrease its number until it reaches 0. If the number reaches 0, we hit the other monster, and we reset its cooldown and start again until one of the monster is not dead.
